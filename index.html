Visual Perception

This module will assess your level of perception, short-term visual memory and interpetation skills.



First you will see a short instruction(1st step) and then 6 simplified instruments (2nd step)
the short instruction will ask you to read the upcoming instruments based on a certain criteria.

1. First find those instruments that comply with the given metrics

2. Second, read where the arrow is pointing at on these instruments

3. Third, enter these digits to the answer field.
